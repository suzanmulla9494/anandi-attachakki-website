Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of atta chakki machines do you sell?

We sell fully automatic, semi-automatic, and commercial atta chakki machines.

2. Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer delivery within Maharashtra and nearby regions. Extra charges may apply.

3. How can I place an order?

You can directly message us on WhatsApp using our product page buttons.

4. What is the warranty period?

All machines come with upto 10 yers manufacturer warranty.

5. Do you offer after-sales service?

Yes, we provide complete customer support after your purchase.

6. What types of grains can I grind in these flour mills?

You can grind wheat, rice, jowar, bajra, maize (corn), ragi, and other dry grains. Some models also support dry masala and pulses grinding.

7. Do the machines require regular maintenance?

No, they are designed for low maintenance. Just clean the filter and hopper regularly. Detailed cleaning steps are in the user manual.

8. Is this machine suitable for home or commercial use?

We offer models for both home use (like Mini Atta Maker) and commercial use (like Commercial Chakki Pro). Choose based on your needs.

9.Is installation provided?

Yes, we provide a complete demo and support for installation at the time of delivery.

10. How do I place an order?
